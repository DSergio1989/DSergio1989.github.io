The border-style Property

This property specifies what kind of border to display:

A dotted border with medium border.

A dashed border with thick border.

A solid border with thin border.

A double border with 20px border.

A groove border with 1px 5px 10px 20px border.

A ridge border with 1px 20px border.

An inset border with inicial border.

An outset border with medium border.

No border.

A hidden border.

A mixed border.
